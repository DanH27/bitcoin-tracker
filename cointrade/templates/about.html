About

8BTCOIN is a ecurrency trading simulator in which you start off with $25,000 and can "buy/sell" coins without any risk of losing money. This is all done in the style of a 8 Bit video game. Currently 8BTCOIN only supports trading Bitcoins, but in the future with support other currencies such as Litecoin and Etherium.
